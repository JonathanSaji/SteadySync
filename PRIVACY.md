# SteadySync Privacy Policy

*Last updated: May 11, 2026*

## Overview
SteadySync is an accessibility-focused browser extension designed to assist users with motor impairments. We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains what data SteadySync does and does not collect.

## Data Collection
SteadySync does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. The extension operates entirely within your browser.

## Local Storage
SteadySync saves your feature preferences (such as whether Steady Mouse, Snap Mode, Voice Control, or Hitbox Display are enabled) using your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Microphone Access
When Voice Control is enabled, SteadySync uses your browser's built-in Speech Recognition API to process voice commands. Audio is processed locally by your browser and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted by SteadySync.

## Permissions
SteadySync requests the following permissions solely to provide its accessibility features:

- **Scripting & Host Permissions** — to run cursor assistance and voice control on the pages you visit
- **Storage** — to remember your settings between sessions
- **Tabs** — to support voice commands that open or close tabs

## Third Parties
SteadySync does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.
