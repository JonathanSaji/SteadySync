🗣️ Voice Commands

Navigate the web using just your voice. SteadySync's robust voice engine works completely offline on your device, ensuring total privacy.

"[Element Name]"
Hover Element
Say the text of a button, link, or input to highlight it. The virtual cursor will snap to it automatically. Example: "Home", "Sign In", "Search"
"Select"
Click Element
Once an element is highlighted by saying its name, say "select" to click it.
"Open"
New Tab
Opens a new browser tab.
"Close"
Close Tab
Closes the current browser tab.
"Steady Sync"
Open Settings
Opens the SteadySync extension popup dashboard.
"Hide"
Close Settings
Closes the SteadySync extension popup.

🎯 Steady Mouse & Snap

Steady Mouse

When enabled, Steady Mouse introduces a smoothing filter to your cursor movement. Hand tremors and sudden jerks are filtered out, creating a fluid, intentional mouse path.

Snap Mode

With Snap Mode enabled, the cursor will magnetically pull towards the nearest interactive element (buttons, links, inputs). This reduces the precision required to click small targets.

Show Hitbox

Turn this on to visualize the hidden magnetic fields around elements. You'll see red dashed boxes indicating exactly how large the clickable area has been expanded.
